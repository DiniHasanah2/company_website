    History Background

    Alumni-Based Startup: Founded by a group of university alumni with a shared vision to create a business that makes a positive industry impact.

    Established in 2019: Focuses on providing advanced system development solutions and specialized training for undergraduates.

    Mission & Goal: Equipping students with essential skills and knowledge for successful careers in industrial and professional fields.

    Steady Growth & Milestones: Achieved significant progress, including an ongoing IoT technology project.

    Strong University Ties: Continues to uphold the spirit and values of the alumni community while thriving in the business world.

    SEELab Research Cluster Support: Played a key role in shaping the company's foundation by providing valuable insights and resources.
